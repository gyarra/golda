GOLDA FOUNDATION FASHION SHOW 2006
SAVE THE DATE…FRIDAY, APRIL 7, 2006

…and join us for a unique, elegant and unforgettable evening of Haute Couture in the lovely Grand Ballroom…

The Golda Foundation presents a Haute Couture runway Fashion Show on Friday, April 7, 2006 at the Grand Ballroom in the Trade Center Building in downtown Fresno.

Silent Auction, Jazz, a No Host Bar & Hors d’oeuvres…wine provided at your table for your enjoyment…

Fashions provided by The Edge Boutique, She She, Mia Bella, Angelica Haley Milan Fashion, Nichole Angelina Grazioli, Designer, and Corey Sprouse, Designer. Hair Design by Eric Gomez.

Details…

7:00pm-10:00pm Friday, April 7

7:00pm: hors d’ oeuvres & no host bar. Opportunity to browse the items for the silent art auction while listening to light jazz…

8:00pm: Fashions on the Runway…wine provided at your table.

9:00pm: More light jazz…silent art auction winners announced.

Hors d’ oeuvres, wine, soft drinks, coffee & tea served all evening…

Getting there…

The U.S. International Trade Center Building is located at 1999 Tuolumne Street at Fulton, across from the Warnors Theatre. Free parking is provided directly across from the entrance.

Event tickets are 100.00 per person. All proceeds go to the Golda Foundation, a non-profit arts & community based foundation dedicated to assisting artists and youth in the San Joaquin Valley…your contribution is 100% tax-deductible…

Sponsorships are available for your business and promotional materials…
