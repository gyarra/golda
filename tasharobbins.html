Tasha Robbins

Tasha Robbins is an accomplished painter. She was living in New Orleans when Katrina hit and forced her to evacuate her studio, leaving much of her work behind. She made her way north. Thankfully, her work was spared by the storm and she is currently living in New York City. tasha_robbins@yahoo.com
