THE GOLDA FOUNDATION’S CHILDREN’S ART EXHIBITION 2005

 On October 29th, the Golda Foundation, held the judging for its first children’s art exhibition at the Valley Oak Elementary School, located in north Fresno.

The goal of the exhibit was to promote the creation and appreciation of youth art, and also to provide recognition for artistic effort within the community.

Grades one through to six participated with over four hundred works produced by the students. Judges chose a first place winner from each of the grades. Each grade winner was awarded a $50.00 cash prize and all students received a recognition certificate at a ceremony at the school on Monday November 14th.

The Golda Foundation produced a calendar of the children’s art work and donated 400 to the Parent’s Club to promote further fund raising efforts for the school. The Golda Foundation will offer this project along with other visual and literary art projects to different schools in the community.

Gala Evening--October 21, 2004
On October 21, 2004, Thursday evening, The Foundation held its first annual gala evening, painting exhibition and poetry competition at the Fresno Art Museum. Winners were announced and awards were given at the Fresno Art Museum. The event also featured poetry readings by competition winners, Elizabeth Sosaya and Dixie Salazar, reading from new novel Prisoners of Truth by Jody Weiner, live music by Tower Jazz Ensemble in the Sculpture Garden, delicious wines and delicacies from Palma Restaurant
