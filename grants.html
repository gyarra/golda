Foundation grants are awarded to artists, 21 years of age or older, for tangible necessities to keep body and soul together while continuing to create their art. Grants are awarded in a gross dollar amount and after considering the Foundation’s resources, the number of applicants, and the purpose and scope of the proposals as they relate to the Foundation’s mission.

The foundation does not fund commercial artists or academic studies. Selection of recipients is made by the Foundation’s Executive Committee, whose criteria will focus on artistic merit and commitment to completion of the work in any medium or media submitted.
