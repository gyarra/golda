The Mighty Schroetter
  -- for Theodore Schroetter - Editor of A Memoir - Vali Myers

--Poet first! Then
grammarian extraordinaire,
sallying forth
with grim precision.—
A declension here,
    an inflection there.
With his surgical sword
he clears the air…
    And need we know?
         And should we care?
You bet we can! And that he dare
disperse each and every
         malingering cloud
where ignorance pulls tight
upon its shroud,
and clings to old habits
dark within, cluttered and clueless,
         which is its sin:
to affront the purity of finer form
and leave it dangling…
like a feast unleavened,
         and a thing half born.
He separates the wheat from that chaff,
and lays them all low with his deadly craft!

Ronald F. Sauer
