The following is a list of 10 books I think might make your life a little more interesting.  What the hell, Oprah is recommending books to 20 million, and she’s responsible for Dr. Phil, which makes her a little less than brilliant, in my book.  On second thought, to save time, since I don’t have a whole lot of it left, let me just give you the authors, and you can make your own choices.  We do have free will, which is only being used by a minority these days.  Not everyone understands what free will is in reality.  That’s because they’re asleep.  A really dangerous occupation is going about attempting to wake them up, as recent assassinations have proven.  There was a whole gang of ancient assassinations, as well.  Some things don’t change all that much.

Haruki Marakami is internationally acknowledged as genius.  I found him addictive, hypnotic and transporting.  The wonder is that you can reread his short stories and once again be transported.  Another wonder is that his books are translated from Japanese.

Daniel Quinn—Ishmeal, My Ishmeal, The Story of B.  Here are books that fall under the heading of ‘revelations.’  Exciting, enlightening, adventures that will literally transform the world you presently believe you’re living.

Barbara Marciniak channels the Pleiadians.  You may resist this idea, however, the information and ideas she presents make perfect sense to someone with an interest in the earth’s origins and futures, enjoys an active imagination and is looking for real answers to everything.

Eckhart Tolle is a real spiritual teacher with a gift for communicating the misapprehensions, challenges, obstacles, secrets, rewards, mystery, blessings, glory and wonder of the inner life. 

The book is Vali Myers, and the author is Gianni Menichetti.  If there is any of the poet in you, and there is some poet in everyone, this book could transport and liberate you, for a brief eternity.
