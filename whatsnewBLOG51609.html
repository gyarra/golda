Death! by Tommy Thompson
 
                                                             Be
 
                                                                  Not
                          
                                                                         Today!
 
 
                                             
                                                 If, I could turn away my eyes
                                                  
                                                 today, from Death tomorrow
 
                                                 ----------- only would I see
 
                                                 Beauty, in every leafen face
 
                                                 and bud beneath -----------
 
                                                 this aged now of yesterday.
 
 
 
                                                 If, I could turn away my eyes
 
                                                 today, from Death tomorrow
 
                                                 ----------- only would I hear
 
                                                 Beauty, sing from birds
 
                                                 and babes -----------
 
                                                 without the screaming
 
                                                 crys of pain from Beauty's
 
                                                 other living things.
 
 
 
                                                 If, I would turn away
 
                                                 my heart ----------- today
 
                                                 from Death tomorrow
 
                                                 and not allow my soul
 
                                                 to grow ----------- to know
 
                                                 that rabbit's fur
 
                                                 in eagle's claw -----------
 
                                                 is unmasked Beauty
 
                                                 without a flaw
 
 
 
                                                 then
 
                                                 Death would be
 
                                                 today
 
                                                 not tomorrow.
